BUG B001
Bug:
- All players in the initial career draft selection appear as OVR 74 or 80.

Probable Causes:
- In `src/game/career/startCareer/startCareer.jsx`, draft generation uses two hard bands:
  - Typical: 68-74
  - Rare: 75-80
- `enforceOverallRange(...)` repeatedly nudges the primary skill until overall fits the band, which tends to push many players to the band ceiling (74 or 80) rather than a spread.
- `targetOverall` is currently uniform, not bell-curved, so there is no center-weighting around ~72.

Potential Fixes:
- Replace uniform `targetOverall` with a bell-curve sampler centered around ~72 for typical players.
- Change overall correction from "push primary skill until in band" to a smoother multi-attribute calibration so generated players settle around target values, not mostly max band edges.
- Add a draft QA guard: if a generated batch has too many max-edge values, regenerate the batch.

Fix:- Change overall correction from "push primary skill until in band" to a smoother multi-attribute calibration so generated players settle around target values, not mostly max band edges.

BUG B010
Bug:
- After match end, app sometimes returns to `/career/start` and appears to lose progress.

Probable Causes:
- Route guard race condition in `src/routes.jsx`:
  - `/match` is treated as debug path unless an active career match exists.
- In `applyCareerMatchResultToGameState(...)`, `pendingConfig` and `activeCareerMatch` are cleared immediately.
- While still on `/match`, guard may detect no active career match and redirect to `/career/start` before/while navigation back to calendar completes.

Potential Fixes:
- Delay clearing `activeCareerMatch` until after leaving `/match` route.
- Or update route guard to allow `/match` when `lastCareerMatchResult` is being committed/navigated.
- Preferred long-term: match end -> `/career/match-summary` route -> then explicit return to `/career/calendar`.

Fix: update route guard to allow `/match` when `lastCareerMatchResult` is being committed/navigated.
- Preferred long-term: match end -> `/career/match-summary` route -> then explicit return to `/career/calendar`.

BUG B011
Bug:
- Match sim balance suspicion: 83+ vs 78 draw occurred too early and feels too common.

Probable Causes:
- In `src/features/matchSim/utils/simulateMatch.jsx`, chance/xG bases are high and variance noise is also high.
- Team-strength impact is softened by divisors and tactical/random volatility, so moderate rating gaps may not dominate enough.
- Additional randomness from chunk-level possession and chance rolls can overwhelm a 4-6 overall gap in single-match samples.

Potential Fixes:
- Increase strength-to-outcome sensitivity (reduce divisors, raise weight of team profile deltas).
- Slightly reduce xG/chance variance noise for closer-to-skill-consistent outcomes.
- Add calibration harness (batch sims by rating gap) and tune draw/win distributions against target ranges.
- Keep upset probability non-zero, but ensure expected win-rate slope is steeper as rating gap grows.

Fix: Ignore this one for now
